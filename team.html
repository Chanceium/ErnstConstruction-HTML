RJ Ernst CEO RJ Ernst, a seasoned Architectural Designer, has made significant contributions to the design landscape. With experience ranging from Owner/Operator at Misener and Ernst to Drafting Co-ordinator at Ridgeview Homes, RJ’s work is characterized by precision and innovation. Their self-employment journey with Ernst Construction & Design further allowed them to shape Norfolk County’s architectural fabric. A brief stint as a Project Coordinator at Acapulco Pools expanded their expertise to the intersection of design and construction. A graduate of Conestoga College in Architecture - Construction Engineering Technology, RJ’s designs, whether on paper or in 3D, tell a story of excellence and passion for the craft.
