Professional Journey RJ is an Architectural Designer with a passion for precision and a knack for bringing dreams to life. I believe in the power of detailed drawings and meticulous planning to eliminate the need for improvisation during construction. My mission is to provide you with a seamless and enjoyable design experience, from the first sketch to the final brick.

I graduated from Conestoga College, where I honed my skills in their esteemed Architecture Construction Engineering Technology course. This rigorous program equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of both the art and science of architecture, enabling me to tackle a wide range of projects with confidence and expertise.

Over the years, I have amassed a diverse portfolio, from building decks to designing multi-unit residential properties. Each project has been a unique journey, offering valuable insights and reinforcing my commitment to architectural excellence.

But what truly sets me apart is my dedication to my clients. I take great pride in building strong relationships with each client, working closely with them to understand their vision and translate it into a tangible, beautiful reality. There is no greater joy for me than seeing a client’s dream brought to life.
